SHOW INFORMATION

Year: 2024

Plot: Former Saratov OBHSS officer Alexander Shishinin, after the birth of his daughter, decides to drastically change his life and pursue his lifelong dream of producing a music group. He meets a talented composer, Vitaliy Okorokov, and together they find the first vocalists, Tatyana Ivanova and Alyona Apina, on the streets, forming the first female band in the USSR.

Release Date: September 6, 2024

Genre: Music, Biography, Drama, Comedy

Country: Russia

Director: Nikita Vlasov

Music: Vitaliy Okorokov, Kirill Borodulev
